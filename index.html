The Vision: More Than Just Cars

At Hot Rod Experience, we believe the core of car culture isn't horsepower—it's people. We are a race fans and community advocates dedicated to shining a positive light on every corner of the automotive world, from grassroots drag racing to high-dollar show cars and everything in between.

Community-Focused

We prioritize respect, positivity, and inclusion at all our gatherings. Every driver, every community member, and every enthusiast has a place here, regardless of their ride or style.

Midwest Roots

Operating out of Indianapolis, we have access to some of the most historic and exciting tracks in the country. Our reach extends throughout the entire Midwest region.

High-Quality Media

We document everything in an authentic and captivating light, allowing our personality to gather as much attention as the content itself. Check out our latest video features.

OUR MISSION

The Core Mission

Hot Rod Experience is dedicated to fostering **Unity in the Car Community**. We strive to eliminate division and spread positive energy at every event we attend and every community we visit.

Race Capitol Home

Based in **Indianapolis, Indiana**—the legendary Race Capitol—our heritage is speed. We cover track events and meets across the entire Midwest, bringing the spirit of Indy with us.

Midwest Coverage

We hit over half a dozen track days every season, documenting everything from grassroots racing to major sponsor highlights. If it's about cars, we are in.
